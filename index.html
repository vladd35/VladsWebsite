Our Strategy

"Our favorite holding period is forever." - Warren Buffett

• On the look for potentially undervalued stocks, so my plan of attack is to hold for the mid-/long- term.

• While generally composed and unphazed by market swings, I am also reactive to disruptive news and the general behavior.

• The rationale behind my choices is based on the pillars below. Naturally, some stocks might not satisfy all criteria:

Relative Valuation

P/E ratio < Industry Median

Forward P/E < P/E ratio

Positive EPS this Y and next Y

PEG < 2 (Company Type dependent)

Intrinsic Valuation

Revenue/Profit/FCF growth over the last 4 Y

FCF substantially > dividends

Current Assets > Current Liabilities

Profit margin > 10%

Some Personal Touch

Where is this industry going, and subsequently, the company?

Who are their partners? Who is at the healm? What are their plans?
